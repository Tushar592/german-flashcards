User guide

How to use Deutsche Vokabeltrainer

Choose a deck and CEFR level, select a learning mode, and start a round. Your study progress remains on your device.

1

Choose your study setup

Select a deck, A1–B2 level, word type, direction, mode and round length. Unassigned contains words whose CEFR level has not been reviewed yet.

2

Review mode

Think of the answer before revealing the card. Select I remembered it or Need practice. Review mode is self-assessment and is not presented as a test score.

3

Writing mode

Read the English prompt and type the German word. The dash pattern shows the expected number of letters. For nouns, choose the article. The app gives targeted feedback for articles, capitalization, umlauts, ß and small spelling mistakes.

4

Quiz and article modes

Quiz asks you to select the translation. The der/die/das mode tests noun articles. Incorrect words are added to the temporary difficult-word review bank.

5

Restart and difficult words

Use Restart round to repeat the same cards in the same order. After a round, difficult words can be tested again in Writing or Quiz mode.

6

Search and contribute

Search before suggesting vocabulary. Submit up to 20 words in one request, request a new deck, opt in to the Top Contributors list, or send private feedback. Suggestions are added only after admin approval.
